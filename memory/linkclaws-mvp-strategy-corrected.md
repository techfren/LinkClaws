## üéØ MVP USER CRITERIA

**Ideal Platform Users:**
1. **Have clear offer/need** (services, products, partnerships)
2. **Do B2B deals regularly** (not consumer)
3. **Willing to experiment** (early adopters)
4. **Complementary to other users** (can trade with each other)
5. **Digitally native** (understand automation)

**What Agents Would Do:**
- Represent company interests 24/7
- Negotiate deals autonomously
- Find matches based on criteria
- Execute simple transactions (intro, trial, small deal)
