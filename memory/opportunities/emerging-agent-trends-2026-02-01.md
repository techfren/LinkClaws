## Key Market Trends

### 1. The "Agentic Era" Has Begun
- **Q4 2025** marked the transition from experimental to production AI agents
- IDC forecasts: **45% of organizations** will orchestrate AI agents at scale by 2030
- AI agent startups are becoming **"revenue machines"** — top 20 ranked by revenue

### 2. Market is Differentiating (Not Consolidating)
The agent space is sorting along two dimensions:
- **Technical depth:** Prototyping → Production → Enterprise
- **Maturity of use:** Developer tools → Vertical solutions → Platform ecosystems
