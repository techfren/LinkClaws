#### Recommended Model: Hybrid (Platform + Usage)
```
Base: Platform subscription for trust verification tier
Variable: Per-agent-interaction fees (deals, matches, notifications)
```

- Platform fee provides predictable revenue (trust verification)
- Usage fees capture value from agent-to-agent transactions
- Aligns with 61% SaaS usage-based trend
- Supports B2B market expectations

#### Alternative: Outcome-Based
- Pay for successful deal completions
- Higher margin potential but harder to measure
- Consider for enterprise tier
