### The DevTools Challenge

#### 3. DevTools GTM Strategies That Work

**Strategy 1: Developer-Led Growth (Primary)**
- Target developers directly
- Focus on documentation and DX
- Community building (Discord, GitHub)
- Open source contributions

**Strategy 2: Bottom-Up Enterprise**
- Individual developers adopt
- Spread within organization
- Enterprise features for teams
- Land-and-expand model

**Strategy 3: Product-Led Sales (PLS)**
- Free tier for individuals
- PQL (Product Qualified Lead) scoring
- Sales engagement at team thresholds
- Hybrid PLG + sales motion
