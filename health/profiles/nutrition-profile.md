Optimization Recommendations

High Priority

-   Switch D3 to D3+K2 combo (ensures calcium goes to bones, not
    arteries)

-   Switch D3 carrier from safflower (omega-6) to olive oil or MCT

Medium Priority

-   Consider increasing salmon to 2x/week for extra EPA boost (your
    algae is DHA-heavy)

-   Add 1-2 brazil nuts for extra selenium insurance (or keep as-is,
    salmon covers it)

-   Consider increasing D3 to 2000 IU based on blood test

Low Priority / Optional

-   Iodine: Occasional seaweed or partial use of iodized salt

-   NAD+ precursors (NMN/NR): Not in Blueprint products --- consider if
    budget allows

*Overall: Excellent coverage. Salmon 1x/week + algae omega-3 + Longevity
Mix + whole foods = very few gaps. Only K2 and iodine remain as minor
considerations. This is a well-optimized longevity-focused diet.*
